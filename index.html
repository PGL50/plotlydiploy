FROM BACTERIA TO BEEF. Improbable Beef is looking for micro-organisms in the human belly button to synthesize proteins for meat substitutes. This website shows the bacteria collected from study volunteers. People can click on the Test Subject ID drop down to see the results of their swabs. Researchers can use the results to see the range of bacteria in all the samples.
